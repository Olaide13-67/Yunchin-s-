Our story

Rooted in science,
grown in Xi'an

Xi'an Yunchin Import & Export Co., Ltd. is headquartered in China's Xi'an National High-tech Industrial Development Zone — the heart of the country's natural extract industry. We specialize in the research, production, and global supply of natural plant extracts and cosmetic raw materials for pharmaceutical, dietary supplement, food, and cosmetic industries.

🔬
Advanced R&D Center
Our technical team operates HPLC, gas chromatography, and magnetic drive autoclave systems, with strong ties to leading scientific research institutions.
🏭
State-of-the-Art Production
Dynamic countercurrent extraction, membrane separation, microwave drying, and spray drying towers form our production backbone — with 900 tonnes of annual output.
🌍
Global Export Partner
From indie beauty brands to established cosmetic manufacturers — we tailor our supply to your formulation needs, anywhere in the world.
